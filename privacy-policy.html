Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how My Building ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our mobile application for building and society management.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects and stores the following information that you voluntarily provide:

  • Building details (name, address, maintenance amount)
  • Flat details (flat number, floor)
  • Resident information (name, phone number, email)
  • Payment records (amount, date, payer name)
  • Expense records (amount, date, category, description)

2. How We Store Your Data

All data is stored securely using Google Firebase Firestore, a cloud database service provided by Google. Data is associated with your building and is not shared with third parties. Firebase's security and privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to:

  • Display and manage building, flat, and resident data within the App
  • Track maintenance payments and expenses
  • Generate financial statements and receipts (as PDF documents)

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics profiling, or any purpose other than the core functionality of the App.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties. The generated PDF receipts and statements are shared only when you explicitly choose to share them using your device's share functionality.

5. Data Retention

Your data remains stored in Firebase as long as you use the App. You can delete individual records (flats, payments, expenses, people) directly from within the App at any time.

6. Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions:

  • Internet access — Required to sync data with Firebase
  • Storage access — Required to save and share generated PDF documents

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
